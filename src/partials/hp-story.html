I lived my first 17 years in different parts of Africa, and for me the baobab represents the whole of that experience. Known as the ‘tree of life,’ it has been West Africa’s medicine cabinet for centuries. Its fruit, bark, leaves, and seeds are a renewable source of health and harmony for natural living. The tree’s deep beauty comes from this model of abundantly meeting the need, and when next to one you can truly feel the magnitude of life. JUJU—which began in my kitchen for myself years ago—uses every part of the plant that is harvested.
